The dataset of “Politics” contains already digitised Swedish Governmental Reports (SOU) and material from the Swedish Parliament, “Media” contains two digitised Swedish newspapers, Aftonbladet and Dagens Nyheter, and “Culture”—which will be digitised—contains a literary journal, Bonniers Litterära magasin and all Swedish novels from 1945 to 1989.
